    What is

    10 Academy

    10 Academy is a
    not-for-profit community-owned initiative
    that has developed scalable, financially sustainable, and effective training programs to place young Africans into careers for the 4th Industrial Revolution.

    Mission

    Building Africa’s workforce in key areas of the 4th Industrial Revolution including Artificial Intelligence, Web3/Blockchain, Internet of Things, and Quantum Computing by identifying, training, and launching the careers of brilliant young people in Africa

    Vision

    10 Academy’s vision is that youth in Africa will have a rich set of pathways to secure decent, impactful, and highly multiplicative employment with social, economic, and environmental benefits.

    Impact

    10 Academy focuses on creating pathways for young Africans to secure high-paying and relevant tech jobs. With a 37.5% representation of women among our graduates, we have already helped 176 individuals across Africa find improved employment. Our commitment remains strong for the next cohort of learners.

    When it comes to job placement,

    95% of 10 Academy alumni secure employment within 12 months of graduating. Notably, all female graduates find employment within a year of completing the program.



    10 Academy covers upfront costs, boosting job prospects. 3 years later, graduates earn an average of $38k/year, amounting to $1.5 - 5 million more over a 40-year career. Our work achieves a 100x ROI and sustains itself through trainees' pay it forward contributions, ensuring long-term impact.
